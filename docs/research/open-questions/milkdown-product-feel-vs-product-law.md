# Milkdown product feel vs product law

## Question

Where should Plate stop following Milkdown behavior even when the executable
proof is strong?

## Current answer

Milkdown is strongest as an executable cross-check, not as the product-feel
authority.

The open question is how far Plate should follow:

- engine-enforced behavior
- versus the stronger product-feel sources like Typora or Google Docs

## Why this matters

Without this distinction, agents may over-weight open-source executability and
under-weight product authority.
